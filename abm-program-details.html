Accountancy, Business, and Management Strand (ABM)

Academic Track

Accounting Business and Management STRAND (ABM)

As a junior high school student, do topics related to business finance, marketing, and economics pique your interest? Well then, Accounting Business and Management strand is probably the academic strand for you! The ABM strand equips students with knowledge and skills about basic practices of financial management and business operations. It also introduces new perspectives about working in the corporate world. Thus, if you are fortunate, explorative, and strategic enough, you could build and manage your own business in the future! As per Aprille Mateo, make ABM your asset, and unleash your entrepreneurial spirit!
