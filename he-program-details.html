Technical Vocational Livelihood (TVL)

Home Economics Strand (HE)

Does anything related to hotel and restaurant services intrigue you? Looking for an SHS strand inclined to that? Worry not, because Manuel A. Roxas Senior High School offers you TVL-HE strand Major in Hotel and Restaurant Services! The TVL-HE Major in HRS trains students with competencies that focus on the key aspects of its industry. Such competencies are food and beverage service; food service management; customer service; industry awareness; and a lot more. The essential knowledge and career skills acquired in studying this strand are needed in either pursuing your college degree or starting your own business or even be employed—backed by TESDA certifications—shortly after graduation. The choice is yours to make!

Manuel A. Roxas High School is a public secondary (high) school located in Paco, Manila
